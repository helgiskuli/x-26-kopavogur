**Definitive source**: each year's operating result is read from that year's own income statement; comparative columns in later years match exactly (no restatement).

**Income-statement "Tekjur/(gjöld) vegna lóðaúthlutana o.fl." (Note 18, A+B, what inflates the operating result)**:
- 2022: 246,217 | 2023: 126,281 | 2024: 3,150,574 | 2025: 4,676,038 (þ.kr.)

**Cash-flow byggingarréttur + gatnagerðargjöld (NOT additive to income-statement line — different presentation, not double-countable per accounting policy note 3)**:
- 2022: 237,713 | 2023: 125,929 | 2024: 3,558,955 | 2025: 6,598,194 (þ.kr., ex. óuppgerðar)

**Accounting policy (AR25 note 3)**: byggingarréttur reversal hits "Aðrar tekjur / Tekjur vegna lóðaúthlutana"; gatnagerðargjöld NEVER hit income statement — they reduce street capex directly. Therefore the *correct* metric for "one-off land income inflating rekstrarniðurstöðu" is the **income-statement Note 18 line**, not the cash-flow sum.
